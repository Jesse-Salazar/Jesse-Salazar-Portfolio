Hey there! 👋
My name is Jesse.
I'm a recent graduate of the Generation USA Developer program.

I'm also a career radio broadcast professional. I sort of fell into it after high school and as it turns out, I'm pretty good at it! I was the funny guy you heard every morning on your way to work, or every afternoon as you were driving home (or to happy hour🥂!) I also held titles on the administrative side as Assistant Program Director (APD) and Music Director (MD). In those roles, I interacted daily with various apps and data manipulaton.

Based on my admiration for programming, I made the decision recently to transition into web development full-time. I successfully completed a fully-immersive four-month dev bootcamp, signed up for several online courses, consistently watch a ton of YouTube videos and continue to educate myself on the ever-changing landscape of software development.

My journey has only begun, but my passion for the industry, along with my drive to succeed and growth mindset will undoubtebly prove to be invaluable for any lucky company or organization willing to invest in my talents. If you're intrigued and ready to see some real code examples...👇
