Welcome to my portfolio!
My name is Jesse and I'm a recent graduate of the Generation USA Developer program.

I'm also a career radio broadcast professional. I fell into it after high school and as it turned out, I was pretty good! I was the funny guy you heard every morning on your way to work, or every afternoon as you were driving home (or to happy hour🥂!)

Although I began my career as a successful radio broadcast professional, I found myself drawn to the world of web development, which has become my new passion. I completed a fully-immersive four-month dev bootcamp, signed up for several online courses, engaged in challenging projects and continue to educate myself on the ever-changing landscape of software development through various resources.

My experience in radio and its administrative side, as an Assistant Program Director and Music Director, has provided me with an array of skills that are transferable to web development, including daily interaction with various apps and data manipulation, and the importance of effective communication. With a growth mindset and a strong desire to succeed, I am excited to continue to learn and grow as a web developer, and to utilize my talents and passion to make valuable contributions to any company or organization that invests in me.
